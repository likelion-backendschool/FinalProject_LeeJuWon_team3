
## 주요 **엔드 포인트**

## 1. **장바구니**

- [ ] **품목리스트** GET /cart/list

- [ ] **품목삭제** **POST /cart/remove/{productId}**

- [ ] **품목추가** **POST /cart/add/{productId}**


## 2. **주문**

- [ ] **주문생성** **POST /order/create**

- [ ] **주문리스트** **GET /order/list**

- [ ] **주문상세** **GET /order/{id}**

- [ ] **주문취소** **POST /order/{id}/cancel**

- [ ] **결제처리** **POST /order/{id}/pay**

- [ ] **환불처리** **POST /order/{id}/refund**
